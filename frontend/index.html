About PM Internship Portal

Background & Objectives

The PM Internship Portal was launched to address the employability gap among Indian youth. It aims to provide practical work experience, enhance skills, and improve the career prospects of young graduates across the country, with special focus on rural, tribal, and underserved communities.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: 21-24 years
  • Education: Bachelor's degree or Diploma in relevant field
  • Family income: Up to ‚Çπ8 lakh per annum
  • Indian citizen with valid Aadhaar card
